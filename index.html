About Me

Biography

I was born and raised in Kristianstad, a place that has always felt warm and familiar to me. One of my clearest childhood memories is spending long summer evenings outside with my family, playing in the garden until the sun finally set. I remember the smell of freshly cut grass and the feeling of running barefoot across the lawn, small moments that still remind me of where I come from and what makes me feel at home. Those early years shaped my love for nature, togetherness, and simple joys.

Academic Interests & Hobbies

My interest in digital tools and creative work has grown gradually over the years. I’ve always enjoyed organizing, creating structure, and finding smart ways to make everyday life easier – both at home and at work. Over time, this curiosity turned into a passion for learning more about digital communication, web tools, and how technology can support creativity.

Outside of my studies, I love anything that lets me express myself or unwind: cooking, baking, traveling, long walks with our dog, gardening, and reading. Food experiences, especially shared with my family, are a big part of my life.

Future Goals

One of my most important goals is to become a front-end developer and work with user-friendly, accessible websites. I want people to feel that technology is simple and inviting, not complicated or overwhelming – and that’s why this goal means so much to me.

Portfolio Highlights

A glimpse into the things that inspire me and reflect who I am – from food experiences to places that feel like home.

Culinary Experience Website Concept

This project explores how high-end dining can be translated into a digital experience. I created a clean, modern layout focusing on visual storytelling through food photography. The goal was to design a homepage that immediately conveys atmosphere, quality, and exclusivity — the same feeling you get when stepping into a fine dining restaurant.

Blekinge Nature Retreat Website

This design concept highlights the calm and natural beauty of the Blekinge archipelago. I focused on a soft color palette and airy layout to create a relaxing and welcoming impression. The page showcases how a location-based business, such as a retreat or vacation home, can use imagery and simplicity to build a strong emotional connection with visitors.

Adventure Travel Promo Page

This project was created as a promotional one-page website for an adventure travel service. The design centers around energy, movement, and exploration. I used bold visuals and strong contrast to reflect the excitement of outdoor activities, aiming to inspire users to explore destinations outside their comfort zone.
